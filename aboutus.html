About Talent Search

What is Talent Search?

Talent Search is an internal platform to discover, post, and participate in short-term opportunities such as initiatives, workshops, events, and collaborations based on skills and interests.

Why we built this

We built this to address fragmented opportunities, underutilized skills, and limited cross-team collaboration. The platform lowers barriers to participation and encourages organic collaboration across teams and disciplines.

What you can do on the platform

  • Discover initiatives, workshops, and events
  • Contribute based on your skills and availability
  • Post opportunities and find collaborators
  • Earn points and recognition through participation
